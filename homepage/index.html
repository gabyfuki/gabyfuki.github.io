Leo's Casa Calamari

My favorites:

  • Grandma Pizza

  • Margherita Pizza

Casa Calamari, previously located on 3rd Ave ad 86 Street, in Bay Ridge was my family's favorite place. It was a beautiful Italian resturant with a homey feel and nice owners. The restaurant was there for my entire childood and it practically watched me grow up. There was almost never a week where I wouldn't eat their pizza.
